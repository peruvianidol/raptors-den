CCBL Power Rankings

As of Apr. 11, 2000

With more games behind us than in front of us, time is running out on some teams playoff chances (including the slumping Raptors!). With 30 games to play, no team has really solidified their position in the playoff race just yet. The top seed in each conference is less than three games in front of the second place team. Meanwhile, no less than 10 teams are duking it out for the final spots in their respective conferences. The Hawks and Sonics put up nice efforts to increase their chances of landing a playoff spot, while Minnesota continues to improve and push for the 8th seed. This year's race should come down to the wire. This week I'm stirring things up a bit by placing less emphasis on overall record. That way some teams can earn some recognition for their recent outstanding play, and other teams (like the Raptors) can be ostracized for their poor efforts.
